Description:

Leaf spot is a common disease in strawberry plants caused by different types of fungi, including Mycosphaerella fragariae and Phomopsis obscurans.The symptoms of leaf spot usually appear on the leaves as small circular or irregular-shaped lesions with a brownish color.The disease is favored by warm and wet weather conditions and can be spread through water splashes, wind, and infected plant debris.

Remedies for Disease

1.To control leaf spot in strawberries, it is important to practice good sanitation, such as removing infected leaves and plant debris from the field, and avoid overhead irrigation, which can promote the spread of the disease.
2.There are several fungicides available for the control of leaf spot in strawberries, including azoxystrobin, chlorothalonil, and captan.
