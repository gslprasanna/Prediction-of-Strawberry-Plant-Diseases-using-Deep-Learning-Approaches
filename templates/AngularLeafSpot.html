Description:

Angular leaf spot is a common disease of strawberry plants caused by the bacterium Xanthomonas fragariae. It is characterized by small, angular water-soaked lesions on the leaves that turn dark brown or black as the tissue dies.The disease is most severe in warm, humid conditions, and it can spread rapidly through the field by splashing water, tools, and equipment.

Remedies for Disease

1.Avoid planting strawberries in areas where the disease has been a problem in the past. If the disease is detected, remove and destroy infected plants and fruit.
2.Copper-based fungicides (Copper sulfate pentahydrate, Bordeaux mixture-a combination of copper sulfate and lime,Copper hydroxide) can also be used as a preventative measure, but they are less effective once the disease is established.
