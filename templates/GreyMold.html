Description:

Gray mold, also known as Botrytis cinerea, is a common fungal disease that affects strawberries. The disease can infect different parts of the plant, including flowers, fruits, leaves, and stems. The symptoms of gray mold on strawberry plants include grayish-brown lesions on leaves, fruits, and flowers, which can quickly spread and become covered in fuzzy gray mold.

Remedies for Disease

1.Gray mold can be managed through good cultural practices, such as maintaining proper plant spacing, avoiding overhead irrigation, and removing plant debris.
2.Several fungicides are available for the control of gray mold in strawberries, including captan, boscalid, and pyraclostrobin.
